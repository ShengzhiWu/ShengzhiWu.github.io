Mindmap

Mindmap is a lightweight, practical and beautiful note-taking software for drawing tree mind maps. Working with the advanced and powerful production tool, with a set of elaborate humanized hotkeys, which can significantly improve your work efficiency, your thought will no longer be limited by the process of recording.

Mindmap contains some advanced features not available in most other softwares. You can easily insert LaTeX formulas and write down technical ideas whenever you want. Inserting HTML blocks is also supported, realising endless possibilities for presentation.

Powered by Electron, Mindmap has good compatibility and is always ready to offer you smooth usage experiences. It also works well with high DPI deviced

No need to worry about the maintainability and extendibility of Mindmap or that your notes may not be supposed in the long term, for the structure of documents (.mm files) of the software has been designed brief, flexible and readable. Even using normal text editing software, you can read and edit the content of your notes. Futher more, in case the tree is not too deep (depth < 7) and has no advanced nodes (like LaTeX nodes), Minmap files is also compatible with Markdown (.md files), which means you can open the notes with Markdown editors (like Typora) to see and edit the hierarchical structure.

Audio Creator

Audio Creator is a software system for audio programmatically generating. It provides an interactive graphical UI, where users can easily construct math and logic filters and processors and get real-time feedback. The main goal of developing Audio Creator is to generate audio for game or film industry.

Compare with the traditional method that recording sound from the real world, parametrically generated sound have the following advantages:

  • Including parameters, which means the sound can changes continuously within a certain range. For example, a program for generating wind sound can has the strength of wind as a parameter. The parameter can take a weather system's output as input and generate sound in real time. Note that to make the sound realistic, it is usually not satisfactory to simply turn up the volume to simulate stronger wind, which naturally means more turbulence and even the sound of colliding sand and dust.
  • Containing more randomization, which improves the naturalness of sound. For example, footsteps can be generated with randomness so that each step sounds slightly different.

Another purpose of designing Audio Creator is to provide a tool for verifying the adequacy or integrality of awareness of the information contained in sound. In my opinion, under normal circumstances, the sign of ultimate (complete) understanding of a system is to use another system with different underlying mechanisms to reproduce the functions. For example, you can't say you understand how birds flight until you've made a mechanical bird that can fly with steel, plastic, and silicon. If you've just saw the muscles and bones of birds and watched a video of a bird in flight, you haven't really understood how they fly. You've only understood the surface. As for sound, only when you have reproduced it with pure mathematical logic, instead of synthesizing it with sound collected in nature, you can assert that you have a complete understanding of all the necessary information it contains. The designing of Audio Creator is carried out under the guidance of this idea.

Grasshopper Tutorial

Grasshopper is a famous 3D modeling software, which is widely used for parametric modeling in CAD fields such as architectural design. With the help of this software and its modern and beautiful interactive GUI, users can easily construct mathematical and geometric logic to build 3D models in a procedural way.

This is a tutorial for Grasshopper beginners. It contains a set of Grasshopper documents (.gh files)。 By understanding and reproducing the examples, learners can quickly grasp the main logic and ideas of the workflow in Grasshopper and gain the ability to complete simple tasks.
